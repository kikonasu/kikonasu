## Design Notes

- **Email 1** uses soft purple/green tones - warm and welcoming
- **Email 2** uses blue tones - informative and feature-focused
- **Email 3** uses amber/yellow tones - urgency without pressure
- **Email 4** uses minimal white design - respectful and clean

1. Reduces persuasion intensity
2. Increases respect for user's decision
3. Becomes shorter and more direct
